Logo for sustainable food app seedbox.

seedbox

This is the logo I made for the sustainable food app project for User Experience module.

The name, seedbox, is combined by "seed" which represents growth and sustainability, and "box" which implies the ingredient kit service in the app.

This is the process of the logo.

The dark green (#217400) represents growth, sustainable and green vegetables.

"Seed" is the main element for the logo. When I was playing with the seed symbol, I found out that the two “e”s inside formed a seed symbol. After adjusting the "e"s, I changed the stroke of the letter "e"s in the brand name to highlight the "e"s. Then, I received feedback that i can move closer the "e"s. I tried, but decided to keep the one above.
